"Quick self-help" by Maya Eya

Sometimes anxiety just comes. No warning. No time. Just that feeling in your chest. In these moments, you don’t need something complicated. Just small things. Simple.

1. Slow your breathing. Try like this:

Inhale slowly through your nose.

Hold a bit.

Exhale slowly through your mouth.

Do few times. No pressure.

2. Come back to now. >Look around. Name few things you can see. Touch something near you. It helps your mind come back a little.

3. Hold something. Anything small. A stone. Your sleeve. Something simple. Focus how it feels.

4. Do something small. Doodle. Drink water. Walk a bit. You don’t need fix everything. Just change the moment a little.

5. Be softer with yourself. You don’t need fight this. Let it be there for a moment. It will pass.

In the moment one small thing is enough. You don’t need perfect. Just something that helps 🤍
