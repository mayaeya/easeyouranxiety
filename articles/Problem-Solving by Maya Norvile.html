🌟 "Problem-Solving for Anxiety and Stress" by Maya Norvile

Problems often feel overwhelming, especially when you're dealing with stress or anxiety. 😬 But there's hope! Below is a powerful guide with simple tools and steps you can take to regain control, even in your most anxious moments.

📊 The Problem-Solving Cycle

Start by naming the problem clearly. Accept that it's okay to feel stuck. Break the problem into parts and take it one step at a time. Plan, act, and reflect — that’s the cycle. You’ll get better with practice!

⚠️ Challenges to Watch For

When you’re anxious, your brain might only see a few options — or none. Don’t worry. This is normal. Avoid perfectionism and give yourself permission to try different ways without judging.

🤝 Building Your Skills

Stay motivated. Breathe deeply. Challenge your thoughts. Instead of saying “I can’t,” ask “What’s one small step I can take right now?”

🔹 Easy Problem-Solving Steps

  • Define the problem
  • List all possible solutions
  • Choose the best option
  • Take action
  • Review the results and adjust

👥 When It Involves Other People

Stick to one issue at a time. Listen as much as you talk. Be kind, be clear, and stay in the present moment. You’re not alone in this.

📊 Long-Term Benefits

These skills will lower your stress, help your confidence grow, and give you tools to handle future challenges calmly and clearly. ✨

You’ve got this! Start small, keep going, and don’t forget to celebrate progress along the way. 🎉
