"How to Find Happiness" by Maya Eya

Sometimes we think happiness is somewhere far. More money. More success. Better life. But even when people get these things… they still don’t feel okay. Maybe it’s not there. Happiness is not in having everything perfect. And not in having more than others. Chasing more can make you feel tired… and empty. Try different way. Slow down a little. Look at what is already here. Something small. A quiet moment. A warm drink. A calm evening. It’s not big. But sometimes… it’s enough.

If you believe in God. You don’t have to carry everything alone. That can bring a bit of peace.

Start very small. You don’t need to feel happy all day. Just one small moment is enough. That’s how it starts. Happiness is quiet. Not loud. Not perfect. Just small and real 🤍
